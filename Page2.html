Catalonia

Northeastern Spain's region of Catalonia is renowned for its unique history, culture, and language (Catalan). Catalonia has a strong regional identity, and there have been ongoing movements for greater autonomy or independence from Spain.

The scenery of Catalonia is incredibly diverse, offering a mix of landscapes from the rugged Pyrenees mountains in the north to the sun-soaked beaches along the Costa Brava. The region is dotted with charming medieval villages, rolling vineyards, and lush forests.

Top Attractions in Barcelona:

  • La Sagrada Familia
  • Monserrat Mountain
  • Costa Brava
  • Salvador Dali's Museum
  • Wine tasting and Catalan cuisine
  • Hike the Pyrenees
  • FC Barcelona Match
